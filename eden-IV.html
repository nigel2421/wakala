Property Description

Imagine waking up to breathtaking views of the Ngong Hills, surrounded by tranquility and fresh air.
Eden Ridge phase 4 offers you this idyllic escape just 30km from Nairobi and 7km from Kiserian town. This 3-acre haven provides the perfect balance between peaceful living and convenient access to amenities.
Here’s what awaits you at Eden Ridge phase 4

  • Spacious plots: Build your dream home on an eighth of an acre, with ample space for gardens, play areas, and relaxation.
  • Scenic views and tranquility: Enjoy stunning vistas of the Ngong Hills and a calming atmosphere, far from the city’s hustle and bustle.
  • Family-friendly environment: International schools like Brookhurst and Olerai are close by, and the Oletepes recreational center is just a 2-minute drive away.
  • Easy accessibility: Tarmacked roads lead you to the property, with a 24-hour petrol station conveniently located at the junction.
Eden Ridge phase 4 Kiserian is an opportunity to embrace a serene lifestyle without sacrificing convenience. It’s the perfect place to raise a family, unwind after a long day, and reconnect with nature.
